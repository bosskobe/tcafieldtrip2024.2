Introduction

The purpose of this document is to provide a detailed account of our recent school field trip to Pampanga, which took place on November 15, 2024. This field trip was organized as part of our STEM curriculum, with the goal of offering students a hands-on learning experience outside of the classroom. During the trip, students engaged in various educational activities, interacted with experts in the field, and had the opportunity to observe real-world applications of the concepts we have been studying. In this report, we will cover the trip's itinerary, the key observations made during the visit, and the insights gained from the experience.

Itinerary

  • 3:00 AM - Departure from Tanauan City
  • 05:00-6:00 AM - Stop Over at Total San Simon
  • 08:00-11:30 AM - Zoobic Safari (Animal Parade and Safari Shows; Croco Loco; Encounter with Tiger; Hip Hop Bay; Savannah; Aeta's Trail; Serpentarium; Bone Muzooeum; Zoobic Park; Rodent World- Mice Surprise)
  • 12:00 PM - Lunch on Zoobic Safari
  • 1:00-2:00 PM- Departure for Subic Bay Rolling Tour (Know and learn history of Subic Bay Freeport Zone)
  • 2:00-3:00 PM - Royal Duty Free
  • 3:00 PM - Departure for SM Pampanga SkyRanch
  • 5:00-7:00 PM - SkyRanch Pampanga (Ride-All-You-Can; Single Ride "Eye")
  • 7:00 PM - Departure from SkyRanch
  • 10:30 PM - ETA at Tanauan City
